Feature plan · 02 / 02

Proposed feature plan. For alignment with Sanket.

Feature MVP · ships for demo Final product · the moat
00 · foundation Onboarding & user profile
Partial Paste CV + 3 goal questions. Agent builds the user profile.
File upload + LinkedIn import. Self-improving profile, auto-updates from messages and meetings.
01 · the wedge Contact discovery
Partial INSEAD directory (placeholder CVs while real download is pending). Top 5 ranked contacts with one-line reasoning each.
LinkedIn extension. Finds contacts at target companies and warm-intro paths through your network. Daily "Today feed" of who to message now.
02 · the moat Context-aware drafting overlay
Out of MVP scope.
Dex-style overlay. Detects open LinkedIn / WhatsApp thread, reads chat history, injects a "Draft response" button inline.
03 · in MVP Voice-matched drafting
Shipped Connection notes, DMs, emails, follow-ups in user's voice. Self-improving from edits.
MVP + voice that adapts to who you're writing to (peer, partner, or senior).
04 · in MVP Meeting prep
Partial One-page memo: company news, strategic priorities, key questions to ask.
MVP + real-time research from web, news, recent LinkedIn posts.
05 · premium tier Meeting recording + transcript
Out of MVP scope.
Built-in recorder, auto-transcribed. Summary saved to the contact's memory. Powers Feature 06.
06 · the engine Relationship nurture plan
Out of MVP scope.
Adapts to relationship strength. Auto-generated after first meeting. Reminders to share articles, send congrats, make warm intros at the right moment.
