Executive read

Romain ran GTM for Life Sciences and CPG at Google Cloud, your Monitor Deloitte world, and is now a founding member of Anthropic's Paris office. He accepted your outreach but does not know you yet, so the goal is simple: learn how GTM actually works at Anthropic France, peer to peer.

Why this meeting matters

A rare contact who holds both your old life-sciences world and your target AI world in one career, and the Paris office is hiring.

What not to do

Do not pitch yourself in the first 25 minutes. Lead with the shared life-sciences ground and curiosity about the build, not your CV.

Suggested flow, 30 min

Profile fit and candid advice.
27 to 30

Who he is

  • Now. GTM France at Anthropic since Feb 2026, founding member of the Paris office, Sales Manager for Enterprise Industries.
  • Before. 13 years at Google Cloud, most recently Head of Sales CELS in France, 150M+ in bookings in FY24.
  • The real common ground. He led Life Sciences GTM at Google Cloud, exactly your Monitor Deloitte world. You both know how pharma buys, prices and launches.

His likely posture

In build mode, hiring, and visible. He responds well to specific, informed questions about the GTM build and to someone who clearly understands enterprise pharma.

Anthropic in France, quick orientation

Anthropic opened its Paris office in late 2025 as a regional hub for commercial growth and partnerships. French customers cited publicly include L'Oreal, BMW, SAP and Sanofi, plus digital natives like Qonto and Doctolib. Note Sanofi, a life-sciences account directly in your wheelhouse.

Romain sits under Thomas Remy, Head of EMEA South, also ex-Google Cloud. The 2026 picture: a big partner-enablement push and a clear vertical strategy with pre-built agents for industries like financial services.
